➤ Details: VOL Health™ story & rationale

VOL Health™ is a directional diagnostic, not a survey or audit. It’s designed to give operators a fast, shared language for how volatility, operations, and labor interact in real buildings under real pressure.

The engine looks at ten drivers – including census patterns, acuity shifts, OT burden, call-outs, PBJ confidence, survey risk, incidents, reputation, leadership bandwidth, partner model, and reimbursement pressure for SNF/PDPM beds – and converts them into a single stability score.

Scores trend into three zones: green (higher stability), yellow (mixed stability / watchlist), and red (high volatility). The point is not a “perfect” number – it’s visibility and language: where volatility is acceptable, where it’s emerging, and where it’s actively extracting value.

The model then pairs that stability signal with an illustrative annual economic range influenced by volatility. This isn’t formal actuarial work – it’s a directional way to anchor conversations about where volatility, survey exposure, and leadership drag may already be costing more than a structured, verified elasticity layer.

In portfolio mode, operators can estimate how many communities sit in each option for each question to create an average per-community view and a portfolio roll-up – without running the tool one building at a time.
